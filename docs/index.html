    About & method

    What this is

    A self-maintaining daily digest of digital-asset regulatory and market developments, written for financial-crime-compliance readers at international banks, covering major regulatory jurisdictions and global standard-setters worldwide. It is AI-sourced and AI-generated: an automated pipeline fetches the sources each weekday morning, and a language model writes the plain-English summaries and implications. Verify against the linked official sources before acting — this page is aggregation, not advice.

    Sources

    —

    Every monitored source and its current status is listed, dated, on the tab. A source is never silently dropped: fetch failures surface there, and persistent failures trigger an automated search for the source's new address.

    Verification levels

    • ✓ Official source Published directly by the regulator or standard-setter named on the card — the statement itself is the primary source.
    • ✓✓ Corroborated An industry-media report confirmed by a second, independent source (an official body or another reputable outlet). Both links appear on the card.
    • ⚠ Single source — verify Reported by one industry outlet with no independent confirmation found at publish time. Treat as unverified.

    What the AI writes

    Headlines link to and quote the original source. The one-sentence summary, the "So what" implication, the category, the priority rating and the "Top of mind" callout are model-written. When AI enrichment has not yet run, cards show the raw source headline and say so.

    Schedule & retention

    The pipeline runs each weekday around 07:00 Hong Kong time; AI enrichment follows within the hour. The Range selector on the Digest tab defaults to Today, with Last 7 days and Last 30 days showing everything this page currently retains within that window, and All showing everything retained with no cutoff at all (none of these are a promise of a full 30 days of history — see below); the Audit log tab records the last 10 data updates. Items older than seven days at ingest are not admitted — this is a daily wire, not a permanent archive. Your Range, Jurisdiction and Category choices are remembered on this device across visits — if the page ever looks unexpectedly empty or dated, check that a wider range or "All" isn't still selected from a previous session, and use "Copy briefing" with that in mind (it copies whatever range/filters are currently showing, not always just today).

    Known limitations

    Some official sites block automated fetching (they appear on the Source health tab as blocked) and their publications will not surface here. Single-source market items may be wrong. All times are shown in Hong Kong time; publication times reflect what the source published, converted.
